Arco Santa Augusta

The Arco da Rua Augusta is a small triumphal arch located in the northern part of the Praça do Comércio in Lisbon . The construction of this arch was planned in 1759, after the destruction of the city center due to the 1755 earthquake. It was designed to be the gateway to the city, but the current version was completed later in 1975.
